## Brand & Objectives

**Brand:** TechEd Academy  
**Industry:** EdTech  
**Primary Goal:** Enroll 500 students in Q2 cohort and establish market leadership

## Executive Summary

TechEd Academy is a results-driven coding bootcamp with 92% job placement rate focused on career switchers aged 25-40. This premium campaign strategy drives enrollment through outcome-based messaging, student success stories, and comprehensive funnel optimization from awareness to application to enrollment.

## Positioning

Career-transformation coding bootcamp for professionals seeking high-income tech roles without traditional CS degrees. Unlike university programs or self-paced online courses, TechEd combines intensive instruction, career services, and job placement guarantees.

## Strategy

Build enrollment pipeline through three-phase funnel: (1) Awareness via success stories and career-change content, (2) Consideration with free workshops and bootcamp previews, (3) Conversion through application support and financing options.

## Campaign Tactics

- LinkedIn ads targeting career-switcher keywords
- YouTube success story series with salary outcomes
- Free "Intro to Coding" workshops (gateway to bootcamp)
- Career-change webinars with alumni panels
- Application deadline urgency campaigns
- Financing partnerships and income-share agreements

## Content Strategy

Educational content on tech careers, learning paths, salary expectations, and career transition timelines. Student testimonials showing before/after career transformations.

## KPI Framework

- Bootcamp applications: 1,200
- Application-to-enrollment: 42%
- Q2 cohort enrollment: 500 students
- Cost per enrollment: <$800

## Enrollment Campaign Timeline

12-week campaign with weekly themes, application deadline pushes, and financing deadline urgency to drive enrollment conversions.

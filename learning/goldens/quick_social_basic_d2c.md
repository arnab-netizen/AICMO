## 1. Brand & Objectives

**Brand:** Glow Botanicals  
**Industry:** D2C Beauty  
**Primary goal:** Increase social media engagement and drive e-commerce traffic  
**Target Audience:** Women aged 25-40 with sensitive skin seeking clean beauty alternatives

## 2. Executive Summary

Glow Botanicals is a clean beauty brand focused on transparency and effective formulations for sensitive skin. This 30-day social media strategy aims to increase engagement by 40% and drive qualified e-commerce traffic through authentic, educational content that empowers women to make informed skincare choices.

## 3. Situation Analysis

The clean beauty market is growing at 7.5% annually, with sensitive skin solutions representing a $2.3B opportunity. Glow Botanicals competes in a crowded space where ingredient transparency and efficacy proof are key differentiators. Current social media presence shows strong engagement (2.4% avg) but lacks consistent posting rhythm and clear conversion paths.

## 4. Strategy

Build trust through educational content demonstrating ingredient science, customer transformations, and transparent formulation processes. Create a repeatable content system around three pillars: ingredient education, customer stories, and skincare routines. Drive traffic through strategic CTAs linking social content to product pages with clean beauty filters.

## 5. Content Pillars

1. **Ingredient Deep Dives** - Science-backed explanations of clean ingredients for sensitive skin
2. **Customer Transformation Stories** - Before/after documentation with sensitivity focus
3. **Daily Skincare Routines** - Morning/evening routines optimized for sensitive skin types

## 6. 30-Day Content Calendar

| Week | Theme | Post Frequency | Key Topics |
|------|-------|----------------|------------|
| 1 | Ingredient Education | 5 posts | Hyaluronic acid, niacinamide for sensitive skin |
| 2 | Customer Stories | 4 posts | Rosacea success stories, sensitivity transformation |

## 7. Platform Strategy

**Instagram (Primary):**
- 5x/week posts: Ingredient carousels, customer stories, product education
- 3x/week Stories: Behind-the-scenes formulation, quick tips, polls
- 1x/week Reels: 30-second ingredient explainers, transformation timelapses

**TikTok (Growth):**
- 3x/week: Skincare science shorts, reaction videos, routine demos
- Hashtag strategy: #sensitiveskincare #cleanbeauty #skincarescience

## 8. Content Buckets

1. **Educational (40%):** Ingredient science, sensitivity triggers, formulation transparency
2. **Social Proof (30%):** Customer reviews, transformation stories, dermatologist quotes
3. **Product-Led (20%):** Hero product spotlights, routine recommendations, ingredient combos
4. **Community (10%):** User-generated content, Q&A sessions, polls

## 9. Engagement Tactics

- **Ask Me Anything:** Weekly Stories Q&A with formulation chemist
- **Challenge:** #30DaysToGlowingSkin challenge with daily tips
- **UGC Campaign:** Encourage customers to share routines with #GlowBotanicalsRoutine
- **Comment Strategy:** Respond within 2 hours with personalized skincare advice

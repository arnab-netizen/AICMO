# NutriBlend Pro – Launch & GTM Strategy

## Brand & Objectives

**Brand:** NutriBlend Pro  
**Industry:** Consumer Electronics  
**Primary Goal:** Successful product launch with 10K pre-orders and strong market positioning

## Executive Summary

NutriBlend Pro is the first smart blender that learns nutritional needs and creates personalized smoothie recipes. This go-to-market strategy targets health-conscious millennials and Gen Z through influencer partnerships, pre-order campaigns, and educational content positioning NutriBlend Pro as essential kitchen technology for modern wellness.

## Launch Strategy

Three-phase approach: (1) Pre-launch buzz with influencer seeding, (2) Launch event with media coverage and pre-order incentives, (3) Post-launch customer testimonials and recipe community building.

## Product Positioning

Smart nutrition technology that removes guesswork from healthy eating—automated recipe generation, nutritional tracking, and personalized recommendations all in one connected blender.

## Go-to-Market Tactics

- Influencer partnerships with 20 health/wellness creators
- Pre-order campaign with early-bird pricing
- Product demonstration videos showing app integration
- Launch event with media and key opinion leaders
- Retail partnerships for in-store demos

## Content Strategy

Educational content on nutrition tracking, recipe customization, and health goal achievement. User-generated recipes and transformation stories post-launch.

## KPI Framework

- Pre-orders: 10,000 units in first 90 days
- Media mentions: 50+ placements
- Social engagement: 500K impressions
- Customer rating: 4.5+ stars

## Launch Timeline

**Weeks 1-4:** Influencer seeding, media outreach, pre-launch content  
**Weeks 5-8:** Launch event, pre-order campaign, retail partnerships  
**Weeks 9-12:** Customer testimonials, recipe community, optimization

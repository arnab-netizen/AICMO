## Brand & Objectives

**Brand:** Meridian Financial Group  
**Industry:** Financial Services (Wealth Management)  
**Primary Goal:** Acquire 200 qualified HNW clients and grow AUM by $500M in 2025

## Executive Summary

Meridian Financial Group delivers personalized wealth management strategies and white-glove service to high-net-worth individuals with $2M+ investable assets. This enterprise campaign targets sophisticated investors through thought leadership, exclusive events, strategic partnerships, and referral networks.

## Positioning

Boutique wealth management firm offering personalized investment strategies, tax optimization, and estate planning for discerning investors who demand sophistication and personal attention beyond what large wirehouse firms provide.

## Strategy

Build trust and credibility through market insights, educational seminars, strategic referral partnerships with CPAs/attorneys, and exclusive client events. Focus on relationship-driven acquisition with 12-18 month sales cycles typical for HNW segment.

## Campaign Approach

Multi-touch strategy combining thought leadership (market insights, investment commentary), relationship building (seminars, one-on-one consultations), and strategic partnerships (CPA/attorney referrals, family office connections).

## Key Tactics

- Quarterly investment outlook webinars
- Exclusive client appreciation events
- CPA/attorney partnership program
- Personalized direct mail to qualified prospects
- LinkedIn thought leadership from partners

- Qualified prospect meetings: 400
- Meeting-to-client conversion: 50%
- New HNW clients: 200
- Average AUM per client: $2.5M
- Total new AUM: $500M

Systematic approach with quarterly themes, relationship milestones, and conversion tracking through CRM system designed for extended sales cycles.

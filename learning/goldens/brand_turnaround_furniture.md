## Brand & Business Context

**Brand:** Heritage Furniture Co.  
**Industry:** Home Furniture & Decor  
**Challenge:** Sales declined 40% over 3 years due to outdated brand perception and quality concerns

## Situation Analysis

Once-respected furniture brand suffering from perception of "dated styles," inconsistent quality, and erosion of customer trust following manufacturing changes. Competitor pressure from both modern DTC brands and discount retailers.

## Turnaround Objectives

Restore brand reputation, modernize product perception, rebuild customer trust, and achieve 25% sales recovery within 18 months.

## Strategic Pillars

1. **Product Evolution:** Introduce modern collections while honoring heritage craftsmanship
2. **Quality Recommitment:** Return to artisan manufacturing with transparent sourcing
3. **Brand Repositioning:** "Heritage Meets Modern" positioning for discerning homeowners
4. **Customer Reconnection:** Win back lapsed customers through trust-building initiatives

## Reputation Recovery

Launch "Behind the Craft" content series showcasing artisans, materials, and quality control. Customer testimonials from long-time buyers. Quality guarantee program with extended warranties.

## Marketing Reset

New visual identity maintaining heritage elements with modern typography. Showroom redesigns with curated lifestyle vignettes. Influencer partnerships with interior designers and home renovation experts.

## Customer Win-Back

Personalized outreach to lapsed customers with exclusive "we've changed" messaging. Special incentives for repeat purchases. Loyalty program relaunch with anniversary recognition.

## KPI Dashboard

- Brand sentiment: Improve from -15 to +25 Net Promoter Score
- Quality perception: 70% "high quality" in surveys (from 40%)
- Sales recovery: 25% increase year-over-year
- Customer retention: 60% repeat purchase rate
- Showroom traffic: 40% increase

## Milestones

- Q1: Quality guarantee launch + new collection preview
- Q2: Showroom redesigns + influencer partnerships
- Q3: Win-back campaign + loyalty relaunch
- Q4: Holiday campaign emphasizing heritage and craftsmanship

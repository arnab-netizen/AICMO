================================================================================
                         PIPELINE DETAILS
================================================================================

STRATEGY BASELINE (5 stages):

   Stage 1: SCAFFOLD
      • Enforces 14 exact sections
      • Fills gaps from brief
      • LLM call: 1

   Stage 2: DEEPEN
      • Minimum 2 audience segments
      • Minimum 3 competitor archetypes
      • Minimum 3-5 strategic pillars
      • Minimum 1 KPI per objective
      • LLM call: 1

   Stage 3: NARRATE
      • Ensures one clear big idea
      • All sections mention big idea + tension
      • Connective sentences for cohesion
      • LLM call: 1

   Stage 4: MEASURE
      • Maps objectives to KPIs
      • Budget rationale
      • Reporting cadence
      • LLM call: 1

   Stage 5: POLISH
      • Removes AI tells
      • Premium language
      • Final QA checklist
      • LLM call: 1

   Total LLM calls: 5 (plus original draft = 6 total)
   Typical cost: ~$0.15-0.30 per strategy (with gpt-4-turbo)

SOCIAL BASELINE (3 stages):

   Stage 1: SCAFFOLD
      • Enforces 9 exact sections
      • Creates 30-day calendar
      • LLM call: 1

   Stage 2: ENRICH
      • 3-5 content pillars
      • Varied calendar (30 days)
      • 8-12 hooks + CTAs
      • Hashtag clusters
      • LLM call: 1

   Stage 3: POLISH
      • Energetic but human tone
      • Removes clichés
      • Final QA checklist
      • LLM call: 1

   Total LLM calls: 3 (plus original draft = 4 total)
   Typical cost: ~$0.08-0.15 per plan (with gpt-4-turbo)
